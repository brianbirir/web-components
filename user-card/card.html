Calvin Wohlert

Over the course of 15 years providing energy services to industrial companies, Waste Hub co-founder and CEO Calvin Wohlert saw his clients paying millions of dollars to dispose of tens of thousands of tons of valuable material. Calvin is an inventive engineer and saw economic, environmental, and societal opportunity being squandered. He wanted to take the material and turn it into compost, or energy, or grind it, or do any number of things to transform the material into something that other companies, even in other industries, would use. He founded Waste Hub to do just that and recruited an enterprising young professional with expertise in operations management to join him.
